Production Notes

Visual Shot Categories

  • Exterior establishing shots of Longyearbyen
  • Library exterior in different light and weather
  • Interior wide shots before opening or during quiet hours
  • Hands, books, shelves, signage, and local history details
  • People entering, reading, browsing, and gathering
  • Ambient town life and Arctic landscape cutaways

Audio Priorities

  • Clean dialogue for every interview
  • Room tone in each filming location
  • Natural sounds: footsteps, pages, doors, wind, snow, town ambience
  • Backup audio whenever possible
  • Careful monitoring with headphones
