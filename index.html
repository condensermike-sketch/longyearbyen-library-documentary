Project Overview

Working Logline

A short nonfiction film about how a public library helps sustain community, memory, and everyday life in Longyearbyen, one of the northernmost towns in the world.

Core Question

What role does the library play in a remote Arctic community shaped by isolation, research, tourism, climate, migration, and seasonal darkness?

Creative Strengths

  • Documentary research and archival thinking
  • Interview-driven storytelling
  • Photography, timelapse, and atmospheric visuals
  • Original ambient music and sound design
